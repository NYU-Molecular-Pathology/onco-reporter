Overview of the Assay

The Oncomineâ„¢ Focus Assay is a multibiomarker NGS assay that enables the detection of variants in 52 key solid tumor genes (Table 1). These genes are well characterized in the published literature and associated with oncology drugs that are FDA approved, part of National Comprehensive Cancer Network (NCCN) guidelines, or in clinical trials.
